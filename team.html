Our Team

Meet the people who will help your business reach its peak

Eli Vida | Owner and Digital Marketing Specialist

For the last ten years, Eli has used social media to drive sales—with promoted products ranging from concert tickets to financial services. He is a content Swiss Army knife. He got his professional start as the Social Media Manager for a Chick-fil-A franchise. He owes his Digital Marketing career path to that experience. Eli’s greatest strength is the ability to collaborate and co-create with maximum efficiency.

If Eli ever powers down, he watches The Office with his partner Kelli, plays retro Nintendo 64 Games, or jams on his ukulele.

Kelli Vida | Account Manager

Kelli has over 8 years of experience in customer service. During the latter half of that time she obtained a BS in Kinesiology. After graduating in 2015, Kelli worked as an exercise therapist for two years. Working one on one with people developed Kelli’s attention to detail and passion to help people achieve their goals. This personal development led to a promotion to Office Administrator, a position she held for over 3 years before her move to the West Coast. With her strong background in case management and client relations, Kelli is our liaison for communications with clients and the digital marketing team. After years of working for doctors as an office administrator, she knows how easy it is for meaning to get lost in conversation, not to mention chicken scratch! Kelli’s goal is clear communication, timely service, and delivered expectations.

When she's not working, Kelli enjoys fine wine and appreciating the artwork of Antonio Segura Donat.

Steven Martin | Digital Marketing Specialist

Still in college, but with no shortage of experience, Steven has been working for the past two years as the owner of INTROSPECTV. He got his start prior to that working at a martial arts school handling their social media marketing and realized his talent and passion for it. He’s a relentlessly hard worker, and his ability to communicate makes him a great asset for our team.

When Steven does finally take a break, you can find him playing League of Legends or binge watching the latest Shonen anime.

Derek Raufeisen | Web Developer and Photographer

Growing up, Derek excelled at numbers and problem solving. Having enjoyed creating products for colleagues, he enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy’s Web Development program where he honed his coding skills and web development abilities. As a freelance photographer, Derek’s clients value his creativity which is a quality he brings to Web Development as well.

When Derek is not at his computer, you can find him playing Ultimate Frisbee™ or rock climbing.

Jesse Smith | Web Developer and Digital Artist/Designer

Jesse is first and foremost an artist and designer, but as a web developer, he aims to bring an artistic eye, with balanced design for UX/UI optimization, to V&P Digital's current and future client base. Jesse is also an avid learner and desires to continue growing as a developer, which is evident by his return to EFA to study software development in 2020. He is thankful for the opportunity from V&P Digital, and is excited to use his knowledge and skill as an asset to their work, as well as continuing to learn and grow alongside the rest of the team.

Beyond development, Jesse enjoys time with family, reading, art and music, seeking out and making good food, soccer, traveling, and experiencing nature.
