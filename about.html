Our Values — What do we value in our team & our clients?



Cooperation

When goals are in-line, the cooperation starts to flow naturally. We’re into the “how do we win together?” mindset. This means clear lines of communication between the whole team working on the projects. Slack is a huge help with connecting your team to ours.

Self Awareness

As we said, we’re very much people. Warts and all. We’re laid back in meetings and anything but when it comes to knocking stuff out.

Goal Orientation

We want to begin each relationship with the question, “What are your goals?” It doesn’t matter who you are, the best work happens when we set common goals first then build the road map second.

Community Driven

“Ethical Business Practices” should be the bare minimum. We want to push team-members and our clients to be reducing harm AND increasing well-being in their sphere.
